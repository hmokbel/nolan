Plaid API Usage

How Nolan Uses Plaid

Nolan uses the Plaid API to facilitate secure bank account connections. Plaid is an independent third-party service; Nolan is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially partnered with Plaid Technologies, Inc.

  1. 1

    User Initiates Connection

    The user taps "Connect Account" within Nolan. No financial data is accessed until the user explicitly consents.

  2. 2

    Plaid Link Opens

    Nolan launches Plaid Link, a secure, Plaid-hosted UI. Nolan never sees, handles, or stores the user's bank username or password at any point.

  3. 3

    Authentication at the Bank

    The user authenticates directly with their financial institution inside Plaid Link. Multi-factor authentication (MFA), if required by the bank, is handled entirely within Plaid Link.

  4. 4

    Plaid Returns a Token

    Upon successful authentication, Plaid returns a short-lived public_token to Nolan's mobile client. This token has no value on its own and cannot be used to access account data directly.

  5. 5

    Secure Server-Side Exchange

    Nolan's mobile app sends the public_token to Nolan's backend API over HTTPS/TLS. The backend exchanges it for an access_token using the Plaid API. The access_token is stored securely server-side and is never exposed to the client or user.

  6. 6

    Data Retrieval and Analysis

    Nolan's backend retrieves transaction, balance, and account data from Plaid on the user's behalf. This data is then processed to generate spending summaries and financial insights displayed within the app.

Security

Data Security

Nolan is built with security as a foundational requirement, not an afterthought. Below are the specific measures in place to protect user data.

  • ✓

    No Credential Storage

    Nolan never collects, stores, or transmits your bank username or password. Authentication is handled entirely by Plaid Link and your financial institution.

  • ✓

    Encrypted Transport

    All data transmitted between the Nolan app, Nolan's backend, and the Plaid API is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

  • ✓

    Server-Side Secrets

    Plaid API credentials (client ID and secret) are stored exclusively on Nolan's backend server. They are never embedded in the mobile app or exposed to end users.

  • ✓

    Explicit User Consent

    No financial data is accessed without a clear, explicit user action. Users must actively choose to connect an account.

  • ✓

    Data Minimization

    Nolan requests only the Plaid API products and account data necessary to provide the app's core features. No unnecessary data is collected.

  • ✓

    Secure Backend Architecture

    All Plaid API calls that require credentials are made server-side. The mobile app communicates with Nolan's backend, which acts as a secure intermediary.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026  ·  Owner: Husein Mokbel

1. Information We Collect

When you connect a bank or investment account, Nolan — via Plaid — retrieves account and transaction data (account names, balances, transaction descriptions, amounts, and dates) that you have authorized us to access. We do not collect your bank login credentials at any time.

Nolan does not require an account, login, or email address. During onboarding you may enter profile details (first name, age, province or state, income, expenses, savings, debt, and goals); this information is stored locally on your device and used to personalize the app. Our backend keeps standard, short-lived technical logs for debugging and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your financial data is used exclusively to provide Nolan's core functionality: categorizing transactions, calculating balances, and generating personalized financial insights within the app. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose unrelated to delivering the service.

3. Plaid's Role

Nolan uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. to facilitate bank account connections. By connecting an account, you also agree to Plaid's End User Privacy Policy. Plaid's privacy practices are independent of Nolan's.

4. AI Processing (Anthropic)

Nolan's assistant is powered by Anthropic's Claude models. When you chat with Nolan, your message, recent conversation history, and a summary of your financial data (such as balances, spending totals, and transaction descriptions) are transmitted securely to our backend and then to Anthropic to generate the response. Your bank login credentials are never part of this data. Under Anthropic's commercial API terms, this data is not used to train Anthropic's models. AI-generated responses are informational and may contain mistakes; always verify important figures against your bank.

5. Where Your Data Lives

Your profile and chat history are stored locally on your device and are removed when you delete your data or the app. Plaid access tokens are stored on Nolan's secured backend server and are revoked when you disconnect a bank or delete your data. Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple; Nolan never sees or stores your payment card details.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to third parties. Data may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate Nolan (such as cloud infrastructure and the Plaid API), and only to the extent required to provide the service. Any such third-party providers are subject to their own terms of service and privacy policies.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The Nolan website (this page) does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics scripts. No personal data is collected through this website.

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit and secure, access-controlled storage for sensitive data. Plaid API secrets are stored server-side only and are never exposed to client applications or end users.

9. Children's Privacy

Nolan is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under Canada's PIPEDA, the EU GDPR, and the California CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to withdraw consent. You can exercise the most important of these directly in the app: Settings → "Delete all my data" permanently revokes Nolan's bank access at Plaid and erases your profile, chat history, and stored data. For any other request, contact mokbel.huss@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app or by updating the effective date above. Continued use of Nolan after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
